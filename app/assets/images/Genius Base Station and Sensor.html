What Are Campaigns?

Campaigns allow you to crowdfund or run a flash sale. An active campaign requires at least one of the 2 goal fields (Sold or Revenue) to be filled. To see it in action, make sure campaigns are enabled. Then save and view your product.

Embed Options

Embed Options

Overlay

Sell directly from your own website with a checkout overlay. Add data-celery="genius" to the desired HTML element, such as a button or image. Remember to include the Javascript tag.

Example: 

<button data-celery="genius">Order Now</button>
<script type="text/javascript" src="https://www.trycelery.com/js/celery.js"></script>

Iframe

You can also use an embedded iframe to sell from your website.

To see an example, check out One Small Business.

<div data-celery="genius" data-celery-type="embed"></div>
<script type="text/javascript" src="https://www.trycelery.com/js/celery.js"></script>
